# Torino Eats - Design Context

## Users
Local and visiting food enthusiasts in Turin exploring authentic restaurants. They're looking for genuine, quality dining experiences - both casual discoveries and special occasions. The moment is often exploratory and aspirational.

## Brand Personality
**Authentic • Elegant • Local**

A refined yet approachable guide to Turin's culinary scene. Not trendy or trying too hard, but sophisticated and confident in showcasing local heritage.

## Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual style:** Elegant minimalism with depth (move away from flat design)
- **Motion:** Smooth, graceful animations - no bounce or snappy movements
- **Color palette:** Granata red (#a81c39) as primary brand color, warm neutrals, clean whites
- **Theme:** Light mode with subtle depth (shadows, layering, micro-interactions)

## Design Principles
1. **Smooth Motion First:** Every interaction uses spring physics (smooth deceleration), no harsh transitions
2. **Depth Over Flat:** Add subtle shadows, layering, and visual hierarchy to break the flat aesthetic
3. **Elegance in Simplicity:** Clean layouts with breathing room, but richer visual treatment through depth
4. **Landing Page Priority:** Hero section needs dimension - consider gradient overlays, parallax, or subtle animations on scroll
5. **Authentic Accessibility:** Respect reduced motion preferences while maintaining sophistication

## Focus Areas for Enhancement
- Landing page: Add depth, subtle animations, visual interest without clutter
- Smooth transitions: All button hovers, card interactions, modal reveals
- Micro-interactions: Restaurant cards, favorite toggle, rating system
