# Torino Eats - Design Context

## Users
Local and visiting food enthusiasts in Turin exploring authentic restaurants. They seek genuine, quality dining experiences without synthetic AI recommendations. Driven by **scoperta** (discovery) and **appetito** (appetite craving).

## Brand Personality
**Minimal • Playful • Authentic**

Clean, unpretentious guide to Turin's real food scene. Trustworthy and slightly witty—like a local friend sharing hidden gems. NO AI-generated content, no trendy gimmicks, no generic layouts.

## Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual style:** Minimal and clean. Every element serves a purpose. Whitespace is powerful.
- **Motion:** Playful but subtle. Gentle micro-interactions (hover, reveal) — purposeful, not decorative.
- **Color palette:** Granata red (#a81c39), off-whites, subtle grays. No pure black/white. No oversaturated accents.
- **Theme:** Light mode (bright, welcoming)
- **Typography:** Geist sans-serif, generous whitespace, hierarchy via weight/size not color
- **What it's NOT:** No AI aesthetics (gradients, glows), no trendy animations, no generic stock imagery

## Design Principles
1. **Clarity Over Decoration:** Every element has purpose. No busy patterns, unnecessary gradients, or filler.
2. **Playful Minimalism:** Minimal ≠ cold. Use subtle delightful micro-interactions without overwhelming.
3. **Authentic & Trustworthy:** Real photos, real data, real users. Zero AI imagery or synthetic perfection.
4. **Local Pride:** Celebrate Turin's food culture. Granata red is non-negotiable. Honor Italian craftsmanship.
5. **Discoverable by Design:** Filter, search, sort, browse naturally. Users discover, not hunt.
